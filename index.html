Come join us!

Western Washington is proud to present the Senior Portfolio Design Show for 2019! We invite you to come and view work from all of our 37 senior student designers. On display will be projects from graphics, motion, web, packaging, user experience and many other areas of design. We encourage you to come and explore all of the work our talented designers have to offer!

Guest speaker

Carson Ting

Carson Ting is a designer based in Vancouver, Canada who enjoys freelance art direction, illustration, mural painting, and app building. An advertising art director, illustrator, and owner of experimental workshop Chairman Ting Industries, Carson has worked with brands such as Microsoft, adidas Originals, Telus, and more. Come and hear Carson talk about his experiences in the industry.
